Our Special Programs

Unique learning experiences designed to inspire and empower every student...!

As a school that drives growth and empowerment in four (4) pivotal areas (children, staff, parents, and communities) we have special programs that we are passionate about:

1. School Agricultural Program: This program teaches the pupils the principle of self-reliance and the value of work. Through this program, they learn how to inoculate growth and develop the necessary patience to tend for and invest the right commitment to take a project from start to completion. Our pupils have planted vegetables, tomatoes, cassava, and cared for these crops. At the end of the process, we review the outcome and everyone is grateful for the experience.

2. Staff Development Program: Each term we engage our staff in a personal development bootcamp. We take a particular book and read together, discussing growth opportunities. We are not just a workplace; we are a growth center.

3. Parent and Community Seminars and Workshops: Each year we organize seminars and workshops for our parents and community. We train them on topics that will empower them to be more, such as money management, health care, business development, and goal setting and prioritization. Our parents and community members appreciate this project and always look forward to the director leading the project each year. We also give materials like clothing and scholarship to individuals in the neighborhood. Also, we contribute to community based projects like road development, security, and so on for the growth and progress of our host community..
