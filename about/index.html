Dominion Harvard Preparatory School is a model which is unique in many ways. It is in a serene suburb of Edo State, providing a conducive and pollution-free environment for optimal learning. The school boasts a team of experienced tutors, with over 60 staff members, who deliver well-structured academic programs and policies. It prioritizes staff welfare, ensuring a stable and supportive community.

The school believes that education is a transformative process that extends far beyond the confines of the classroom. Its approach is designed to foster a deep understanding of God, self, and the world, empowering students to become well-rounded, effective, and responsible citizens. It strives for inclusivity, providing equal access to literacy and essential life skills programs for all children, regardless of background, race, gender and demographic characteristics.

The school environment is characterized by warmth, serenity, and a commitment to the holistic development of the students. It prioritizes their physical, psycho-social, moral, and spiritual growth, ensuring a safe and secure space for all ages. The management team is goal-driven, efficient, and visionary, providing memorable learning experiences that shape social life, self-concept, and occupational futures.
